Urban Transformation Project

Goal of the project

The goal of the project is to create conditions for the systematic improvement of the quality and comfort of the urban environment based on requests and initiatives of residents.

Landscaped parks, bike paths, lighted streets, pedestrian zones - all this should be in any city. Only in this case people will like to live and work there. This is the approach laid down in the priority project "Urban Transformation Project".
